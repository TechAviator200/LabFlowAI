RT-qPCR Gene Expression Protocol — SYBR Green Method
Lab: Molecular Biology Core
Version: 1.4
Last Updated: 2025-01-08

Purpose:
Quantify relative mRNA expression using RT-qPCR with SYBR Green detection. Reference gene normalization (GAPDH or ACTB) is required for all expression data.

Equipment:
- Applied Biosystems QuantStudio 6 Flex Real-Time PCR System
- MicroAmp optical 96-well plates
- Thermal sealer
- Micropipettes and filtered tips

Reagents:
- SYBR Green PCR Master Mix (2x) — Applied Biosystems 4309155
- Forward and reverse primers (10 µM working stock)
- RNase-free water
- cDNA template (prepared from RNA extraction protocol SOP-RNA-001)

RNA Extraction (separate SOP):
Ensure RNA is of sufficient quality (RIN > 7, A260/280 > 1.8) before proceeding.

cDNA Synthesis:
Reverse transcribe 500 ng–1 µg total RNA using SuperScript IV VILO Master Mix per manufacturer instructions. Dilute cDNA 1:5 in RNase-free water before use in qPCR.

qPCR Setup:
1. Prepare master mix per reaction:
   - 10 µL SYBR Green Master Mix (2x)
   - 1 µL forward primer (10 µM)
   - 1 µL reverse primer (10 µM)
   - 7 µL cDNA + RNase-free water

2. Dispense 20 µL total per well into 96-well optical plate.

3. Add 2 µL cDNA template per well (from diluted stock).

4. Include: no-template controls (NTC) and reference gene reactions on every plate.

5. Seal plate tightly with optical adhesive film.

Thermal Cycling Conditions:
- Initial denaturation: 95°C for 10 minutes
- 40 cycles:
  - Denaturation: 95°C for 15 seconds
  - Annealing/Extension: 60°C for 1 minute
- Melt curve: 95°C 15s → 60°C 1 min → 95°C 15s (continuous)

Data Analysis:
- Use ΔΔCt method for relative quantification
- Normalize to reference gene (GAPDH preferred)
- Report fold-change relative to control condition
- Verify single-peak melt curve for all amplicons

Expected Outputs:
- Ct values for each well
- Melt curve data
- Amplification efficiency per primer pair
- Calculated ΔCt and ΔΔCt values
- Fold-change expression data

Acceptance Criteria:
- NTC wells: no amplification (or Ct > 35)
- Duplicate Ct values: CV < 5%
- Melt curve: single peak (no secondary peaks)
